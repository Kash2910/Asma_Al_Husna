Core Principles of Understanding

The Holistic Meaning of "Ahsaha"

The Hadith promising Paradise to one who "knows" (ahsaha) the 99 names implies more than mere rote memorization. Scholars describe a four-step process of deep spiritual engagement:

1. Memorize the words
→
2. Understand their meanings and implications
→
3. Supplicate to Allah with them
→
4. Act upon what they necessitate
